Reflection

Reflection 1: In-Class Exercise

Through this in-class exercise, my group members—Zi Ning, Sarves, and Yaash—and I explored the role of phospholipids in the cell wall. The activity made the class more engaging and interactive, allowing us to create simplified notes for better revision. It was a valuable experience that enhanced my understanding of the topic while fostering collaboration with my peers.

Reflection 2: Introduction to DNA Transcription

Together with my group members—Kiff, Anisah, and Qira—we discussed the key content to include in our mind map about the subtopic of genetic information and transcription. Using Canva, we collaboratively created a detailed and visually appealing mind map. This exercise deepened my understanding of the subtopic, and I enjoyed working with my teammates, strengthening our relationships in the process.

Reflection 3: 20 Different Types of Amino Acids

Our group engaged in a discussion on the diversity of amino acids, their chemical properties, and how this diversity contributes to a wide range of protein functions. We also researched the genetic coding of amino acids, along with their one-letter and three-letter abbreviations. To summarize our findings, we designed a comprehensive mind map using Canva. This discussion was a fantastic opportunity to gain a deeper understanding of this subtopic and improve my collaborative skills.
