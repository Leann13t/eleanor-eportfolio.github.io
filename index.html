23 December 2024

Industry Talk:
Skills in University and Industry

On 23 December 2024, our faculty organized an industry talk titled Skills in University and Industry, featuring two speakers: Encik Mohd Hakimi Iqmall (Project Manager/System Analyst) and Encik Nik Mohd Habibullah Bin Nik Mohd Nizam. Encik Hakimi delivered a session titled Designing Success: From Graphic Multimedia to Leading Projects, while Encik Nik hosted Preparing for Entrepreneurship in IT: Essential Skills and Strategies for University Students.

Full Story

Encik Hakimi, currently working at UTM Digital as a Project Manager and System Analyst, oversees various projects, including the Welfare Services System, Clinic Panel System, Kenaikan Gaji Tahunan (KGT), Payroll 2.0, SSPA System, and Integrity System. He emphasized that achieving success in the IT field requires a combination of a strong technical foundation and comprehensive management skills. These skills are essential for aligning stakeholder expectations, mitigating risks, ensuring quality assurance, and maintaining project coherence. Encik Hakimi highlighted the importance of continuous learning, particularly in mastering emerging technologies, to stay aligned with industry trends and meet client demands. He encouraged individuals to identify their career interests and step beyond their comfort zones to foster personal growth, drive innovation, and adapt to the rapidly evolving IT landscape.
Encik Nik, the CEO of Micro Semiconductor Sdn. Bhd., introduced GetMe Hired, a platform he co-founded to support fresh graduates in creating professional CVs and enhancing their employability. He concluded that success in IT and related fields requires a clear career vision, a commitment to lifelong learning, and continuous skill development. Mastery of role-specific competencies, coupled with the strategic use of tools like Lucidchart for system modeling, Microsoft Project for project management, and Adobe Creative Suite for design, is essential for building a robust technical foundation. By integrating these tools with thoughtful planning and a proactive mindset, individuals can effectively adapt to industry demands and achieve sustained professional growth.
