Reflection Case Study: "RASUAH TAMBAH MARKAH PENSYARAH MATEMATIK"

In the Integrity and Anti-Corruption Course, our group chose the case study topic: "Rasuah Tambah Markah Pensyarah Matematik" (Bribery to Increase Mathematics Lecturer's Marks). We were assigned to create a poster and video based on this topic. As part of the project, we gathered extensive data on integrity, corruption, and its impact. Our research included exploring why such situations arise, potential solutions to address them, and initiatives that can be implemented to prevent such unethical behavior.
The highlight of our project was filming a video that depicted a real-life scenario. In the video, we portrayed various characters, including a professor accepting bribes, students offering bribes, and others affected by the situation. Through this dramatization, we showcased the severe consequences of engaging in corruption and highlighted the importance of ethical behavior.
This experience was not only informative but also deeply impactful. We were able to learn about the practical implications of integrity and the damaging effects of corruption on individuals and society. Additionally, the project helped us improve our teamwork, communication, and creative thinking skills. Acting out the scenario and creating visual content enhanced our understanding of the topic and made the learning process enjoyable. Overall, this assignment provided a valuable opportunity to gain insights into ethical behavior and the importance of promoting integrity in all aspects of life.
